Thankyou for Registering RPManager/RPManagerLite

To complete registration you will need one or more Licence Keys depending on quantity purchased - these are generated based on the HardwarelockID(s) of the 3dmax installation(s) you wish to register RPManager/RPManagerLite with.
In addition if you are using flexlm for your 3dsmax licensing and are not purchasing a site license you will need to provide the computername that each license will be tied to - the HardwarelockID and computername can be quickly found using the 'Authorize Info...' menu item in the RPM dialogs Help menu (this dialog also indicates whether computer name is needed for licensing). It is very important that the case and structure of the computer name is correct - copying and pasting from the 'Authorize Info' into the email being sent to licensing@rpmanager.com is the recommended way to get it right and reduce delays.

The safest way to collect the correct information required is to use the 'Save Date to Text File' button in the 'Authorize Info...' dialog and email that file or files to licensing@rpmanager.com.

Upon receipt of your order you will be contacted by licensing@rpmanager.com at the email used to purchase RPManager/RPManagerLite. If you wish to have the licensing dealt with through a different email please contact licensing@rpmanager.com as soon as possible with your alternate details - please include the purchase/purchasers details to verify that it is a valid request.

The email from licensing@rpmanager.com will request the relevant information to create licenses for your RPManager installation.

Thankyou for your support. Please contact licensing@rpmanager.com with any questions about licensing.
